Blueprint for 3-Month Execution

A rapid, iterative approach is key. Leverage no-code platforms to move from idea to data collection in weeks, not months.

1

Weeks 1-2: Prioritize & Plan

Identify 2-3 high-frequency use cases (e.g., appointment booking, FAQ automation). Define key data points to capture and design the core conversation flow on a no-code platform like Voiceflow or Echo.win.

2

Weeks 3-6: Build & Deploy MVP

Develop and launch the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) voice agent. Integrate with necessary tools (Calendars, CRMs) and begin collecting initial user interaction data. Focus on stability and core functionality.

3

Weeks 7-12: Iterate & Scale

Analyze transcripts, sentiment, and user journeys. Use insights to refine conversational flows, improve objection handling, and expand the agent's knowledge base. Scale the deployment to a wider user base to maximize data volume.
